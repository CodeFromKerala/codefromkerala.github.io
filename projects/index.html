SimplyCalc

Collaborators: Just Me

A poorly-named, Coca-Cola fueled, C library which is supposed to help model fractional derivatives and integrals.

I haven’t used AI in it, so it’s bound to have mistakes. Please use at your own risk.

On a more serious note, it’s based on a paper I’m working on currently on the algebraic origins of fractional calculus. I want to eventually build this to a good standard and test errors between my truncated infinite series forms and the actual integrals (Riemann-Liouville Integral and Caputo Derivative).
